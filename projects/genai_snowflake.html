GenAI - Snowflake

  • Tech Stack: Snowflake Cortex, Snowpark, SQL, Snowflake, LLMs (llama3-8b, mistral-7b, mistral-large, mistral-7b (Finetuned))
  • Github URL: Project Link

This project harnesses advanced LLMs to quickly parse call transcripts. It extracts actionable insights by analyzing sentiment, categorizing issues, and summarizing conversations. The outcome is a faster, more personalized response process that significantly reduces manual effort.

Snowflake coordinates secure data ingestion from sources like S3 and orchestrates the AI-driven analysis. Our models, including Mistral-7b is fine-tuned to handle the nuances of natural language. Streamlit then delivers a user-friendly interface that keeps everything accessible and efficient.

This approach drastically shortens resolution times and improves customer satisfaction. It offers a scalable blueprint for transforming contact centers into agile, data-driven environments. By automating repetitive tasks, teams can focus on higher-value engagement that elevates the overall experience.
